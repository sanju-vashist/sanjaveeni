About Saanjavni

Saanjavni is a comprehensive medical information app designed to simplify healthcare management. It offers features such as biometric registration, personal and family medical information management, emergency access, and a user-friendly dashboard.

Creators

This app was developed by a dedicated team of engineers and developers. Our goal is to revolutionize how medical information is managed, making it more accessible and secure.

Our Motive

The motive behind Saanjavni is to provide a platform that empowers users to take control of their health information. By integrating biometric security and comprehensive medical data, we aim to make healthcare more efficient and user-centered.

Features

  • Biometric registration with face and fingerprint recognition
  • Personal and family medical information management
  • Emergency access features for critical situations
  • User-friendly dashboard inspired by Snapchat's design
